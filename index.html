Our Restaurant

Welcome to Crave Kitchen, a culinary oasis bringing the authentic flavors of Kenya to your table. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a celebration of Kenyan culture, warmth, and community. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, Crave Kitchen invites you on a tantalizing journey through Kenya's rich culinary heritage, where every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and love.

Menu

At Crave Kitchen, we strive to offer an unforgettable dining experience that encapsulates the vibrant essence of Kenya. Our chefs meticulously craft each dish, infusing it with locally sourced ingredients and age-old recipes passed down through generations. From the aromatic spices of coastal dishes to the hearty, comforting flavors of the highlands, our menu reflects the diversity and richness of Kenyan cuisine.

Meet Our Chefs

Wanjiku Njoroge - Master of Kenyan Fusion

Hailing from the slopes of Mount Kenya, Chef Wanjiku Njoroge brings a wealth of expertise in fusing traditional Kenyan recipes with contemporary culinary techniques. With a keen eye for detail and an innate understanding of local flavors, Chef Wanjiku's innovative touch infuses each dish with a delightful modern twist, ensuring every bite at Crave Kitchen is a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation.

Chef Omondi Kariuki - The Maestro of Coastal Delights

Born and raised along the sun-kissed shores of Mombasa, Chef Omondi Kariuki's culinary journey is a tribute to the coastal wonders of Kenya. With a deep-rooted passion for seafood and the aromatic spices of the Swahili coast, Chef Omondi's creations at Crave Kitchen effortlessly transport diners to the vibrant beaches of Kenya, tantalizing their palates with the bold, tantalizing flavors of the Indian Ocean.

Chef Achieng Odhiambo - The Guardian of Kenyan Traditions

Embodying the spirit of Kenyan tradition, Chef Achieng Odhiambo is the guardian of age-old recipes and authentic culinary practices. Hailing from the heart of Nairobi, her culinary prowess lies in recreating the comforting, soul-nourishing flavors of Kenyan home-cooked meals.
